SMS & OTP Usage Policy

AgroMath uses SMS strictly for transactional and security-critical communication, including:

  • One-Time Passwords (OTP) for login and account verification
  • Password reset and account recovery codes
  • Order confirmations and delivery status notifications
  • Escrow, dispute, and service-critical alerts

AgroMath does not send promotional or unsolicited marketing SMS.

Who Can Use AgroMath

• Farmers listing bulk agricultural products
• Buyers sourcing produce at wholesale scale
• Transporters quoting and fulfilling delivery requests

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgroMath?

AgroMath is a wholesale agricultural marketplace that connects farmers, bulk buyers, and transporters to trade and coordinate logistics more efficiently.

Who can use AgroMath?

AgroMath is built for three user groups: Farmers (sellers), Buyers (bulk purchasers), and Transporters (logistics providers).

How do I create an account and log in?

Use your phone number to register and log in. A one-time password (OTP) is used to verify access and improve account security.

How do buyers place orders?

Buyers browse listings, select quantity, and submit an order request. The farmer reviews the request and confirms availability before fulfillment proceeds.

How is delivery handled?

Delivery is coordinated through transporters where available. Costs and logistics are agreed during order coordination.
