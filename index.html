Wholesale Agricultural Trading, Simplified

AgroMath is a trust-driven wholesale marketplace connecting farmers, buyers, and transporters for bulk agricultural trade. The platform enables verified listings, logistics coordination, and transparent order fulfillment from farm to city.

SMS & OTP Usage Policy

AgroMath uses SMS strictly for transactional and security-critical communication. This includes:

  • One-Time Passwords (OTP) for login and account verification
  • Password reset and account recovery codes
  • Order confirmations and delivery status notifications
  • Escrow, dispute, and service-critical alerts

AgroMath does not send promotional, bulk, or unsolicited marketing messages. All SMS communication is user-initiated or service-critical.

Who Can Use AgroMath

• Farmers listing bulk agricultural products
• Buyers sourcing produce at wholesale scale
• Transporters quoting and fulfilling delivery requests
